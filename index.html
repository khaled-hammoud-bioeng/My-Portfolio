Senior Project · LIU

Smart Tracking System
for Diabetic Foot Monitoring

A clinically deployable wearable system engineered to give diabetic patients a proactive tool for managing foot health in real time. By mapping plantar pressure and monitoring tissue temperature continuously, the system enables early detection of ulceration and neuropathic risk — conditions linked to over 80,000 lower-limb amputations annually worldwide.
